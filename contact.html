FAQ

Is membership free?+
Yes — completely free. There are no dues, no fees, and no hidden costs. All workshops, events, and sessions are open to registered members at no charge.
Do I need prior experience to join?+
Not at all. We welcome everyone from complete beginners to advanced students. Our tracks are designed to meet you where you are — just show up curious.
Can I join multiple tracks?+
Absolutely. There's no limit to how many tracks you participate in. Many members attend both algorithm sessions and web development workshops each week.
Is the club only for CS students?+
No. While most members study CS or Software Engineering, we welcome students from any department. A curiosity for technology is all you need.
How do I sign up?+
Fill out the contact form on this page, send us an email, or just show up to a Wednesday meetup at B-204. We'll handle the rest from there.
